### Getting Started

#### 1. Create an Account
1. Visit the website and click **"Sign Up"**.

### Booking a Property

#### Request a Viewing
1. On the property page, click **"Book Viewing"**.
2. Select a date/time slot.
3. Add a message to the landlord.
4. Submit request. You will be notified via email when approved.

#### Complete a Rental
1. Once approved, navigate to **My Bookings**.
2. Wait for the Landlord to initiate the **Digital Agreement**.
3. You will receive an email notification: *"Action Required: Sign Lease Agreement"*.

---

### Managing Agreements

#### Digital Signature Workflow
1. Go to **My Agreements**. Status will show `PENDING_TENANT`.
2. Click **"Sign Now"**.
3. Review the PDF terms generated by our system.
4. Draw your e-signature in the canvas box.
5. Click **"Confirm Signature"**.
   - *Note: Your IP address and timestamp are cryptographically hashed with the signature for legal non-repudiation.*
6. Download the final signed PDF immediately.
